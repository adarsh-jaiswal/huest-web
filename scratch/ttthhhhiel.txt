When a student or a working professional , have to move to a new location for study or work,  it's very difficult for them to find a decent place to live and home cooked food.In India ,there are a lot of students who don't want to stay in hostel for a number of reasons.I am building a website and a mobile application HUEST where student/working professionals will be treated as GUEST and the people who will be providing accommodation and food facility will be the HOST. with the help of Huest:-
1.Guest can login through his app and find host near his/her required location,can give feedback about the host.
2. Host can register the details about the type of accommodation and food facility and the charges. If the host can only provide food facility he/she can also register. 
With the help of Huest a large number of indian housewives can earn money while sitting at their home,plus the students and the working professionals can find accommodation while sitting at their current location before moving.
the prototype is in development phase .
