welcome to huest!

Finding a place to stay which also provides you with home cooked food had never been so easy. We provide you a platform where we help you find either a host or a guest.You are a host if you want to provide accomodation or food facility or both.You are a guest if you are looking for a place to stay in a new location which also provides you with home cooked food.


Awesome Features

Get a Host,Anywhere,Anytime

Whenever you have to move to a new loaction find a host by sitting in your home who will provide you with the accomodation or food facility or both at one place.

Become a Host

Have a place which you want to rent for the guest or provide food or both.Housewives have a great oppurtinity to just serve food to the guest and earn money.

Contact your host/guest

With the help of Huest both the guest and the host can directly contact each other and if all goes well then they can make a deal.
